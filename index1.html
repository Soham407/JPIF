About ACIC-JPIF

Jnana Prabodhini Innovation Foundation is a Section 8 company promoted by Jnana Prabodhini in collaboration with the Atal Innovation Mission (AIM), NITI Aayog, Government of India. The Atal Community Innovation Center (ACIC) initiative aims to focus on nurturing grassroots innovators, rural entrepreneurs, and community-led enterprises from underserved regions.

ACIC-JPIF will serve as a catalyst for transforming innovative ideas into sustainable enterprises that create measurable social and economic impact.

About Jnana Prabodhini

Jnana Prabodhini is a reputed social institution with over six decades of experience in rural development, entrepreneurship development, women empowerment, biotechnology, agriculture, and natural resource management across Maharashtra.

Our Vision

  • Promote grassroots innovation and entrepreneurship in underserved communities.
  • Support startups aligned with Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
  • Enable rural innovators to build financially sustainable enterprises.
  • Create an inclusive innovation ecosystem connecting communities, institutions, mentors, and markets.

Our Mission

  • Identify and nurture innovators from rural and semi-urban areas.
  • Build entrepreneurship capabilities among youth, women, SHGs, farmers, FPOs, and community organizations.
  • Provide incubation, mentoring, technical, and business support to startups.
  • Facilitate market access, partnerships, and funding opportunities.
  • Develop scalable solutions in agriculture, biotechnology, renewable energy, sustainability, and health sectors.

Bottom-Up Community Incubation

The incubation model is designed around a bottom-up community entrepreneurship approach, specifically catering to underserved collective groups and local talent pools.

Key Target Groups

  • Self Help Groups (SHGs)
  • Farmers and Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs)
  • Rural Youth
  • Students & Researchers
  • Entrepreneurs
  • Voluntary Organizations
  • Ayurvedic Practitioners

Key Interventions

  • Design Thinking Workshops
