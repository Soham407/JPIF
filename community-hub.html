Community Entrepreneurship Programs

We empower target beneficiary groups with core skills, technical access, and market linkages.

Self Help Groups (SHGs)

Transition saving collectives into commercial enterprises.

  • Enterprise Development: Structured path to corporate registry.
  • Value Addition Activities: Sorting, grading, and basic processing setup.
  • Product Development: Design, testing, and standard certifications.
  • Financial Literacy: Capital accounting, compliance, and banking links.

Farmers & FPOs

Empower agricultural collectives with business scaling.

  • Agri Entrepreneurship: Processing unit models and cold logistics.
  • Processing Enterprises: Milling, packaging, and sorting setups.
  • Market Linkages: Direct B2B retail links and wholesale network access.
  • Technology Adoption: Mechanical tools and soil data setups.

Bamboo Entrepreneurs

Leverage rural bamboo resources for sustainable products.

  • Bamboo Cultivation: Crop management and plantation frameworks.
  • Nursery Development: High-yield crop propagation.
  • Processing Units: Treatment, splitting, and sizing machinery.
  • Product Manufacturing: Structural, craft, and industrial builds.
  • Market Access: Wholesale commercial purchase contracts.

Women Entrepreneurs

Support home-based micro-enterprises to scale up.

  • Home-Based Enterprises: Cottage processing, packaging, and assembly checks.
  • Rural Business Development: Local sales modeling and vendor setups.
  • Skill Enhancement: Technical workshops and product refinement.
  • Leadership Development: Financial control and team management training.

Rural Youth

Provide local livelihood opportunities in rural areas.

  • Startup Development: Launching service and trading operations.
  • Innovation Competitions: Rural hackathons and project awards.
  • Entrepreneurship Training: Business planning and sales pitches.
  • Livelihood Opportunities: Technical jobs creation locally.

Students & Researchers

Translate academic innovations into commercial products.

  • Innovation Commercialization: Transitioning lab mockups to fields.
  • Startup Creation: Incorporations and co-founder mapping.
  • Research Translation: Pilot deployments and field performance audits.
  • Product Development: Material optimization and lab certification checks.

Core Program Interventions

Our incubation models deploy structured interventions across target groups: Entrepreneurship Development Programs (EDPs) • Technical & Skill Development Workshops • Design Thinking Sessions • Dedicated Business Mentoring • Direct Technology Transfer • Access to Finance & Micro-credit • Direct Market Linkages • Incubation Advisory Support.
