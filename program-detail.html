Pre-Incubation Program

Converting Ideas into Entrepreneurial Opportunities. The Pre-Incubation Program is designed for individuals and teams who have identified a problem or opportunity but require support to shape their ideas into viable business concepts. This serves as the first step in the entrepreneurial journey.

Program Objectives

  • Foster Innovation Thinking & Promote Entrepreneurship Culture.
  • Identify High-Potential Ideas & Build Core Capabilities.
  • Prepare Participants for active Physical or Virtual Incubation.

Program Components

  • Ideation Workshops: Problem Identification, Opportunity Mapping, and Idea Generation.
  • Design Thinking: User-Centric Innovation, Empathy Mapping, and Rapid Prototyping.
  • Innovation Challenges: Ideathons, Hackathons, and Community Problem Solving.
  • Entrepreneurship Awareness: Business Basics, Startup Fundamentals, and Market Understanding.
  • Expert Interactions: Sessions with Entrepreneurs, Industry Leaders, and Successful Innovators.

Expected Outcomes

Refined Business Idea • Preliminary Business Model • Empathic Customer Understanding • Incubation Readiness

Physical Incubation (10 - 12 Months)

Transforming Ideas into Sustainable Enterprises. The Physical Incubation Program is designed for innovators, startups, community entrepreneurs, SHGs, FPOs, rural youth, researchers, and enterprises requiring intensive handholding and access to physical infrastructure in Pune and Nigdi. The program focuses on nurturing innovations from the idea stage to commercialization.

Incubation Levels

LEVEL 1

Idea to Proof of Concept

Supports innovators in validating their ideas and developing initial prototypes.

Support Offered:

  • Design Thinking Workshops
  • Problem Identification & Validation
  • Product Concept Development
  • Access to Domain Experts & Makerspace
  • Prototype Development Support
  • Market Need Assessment
  • Initial Business Model Development

Expected Outcomes:

  • Validated problem statement
  • Working prototype or Proof of Concept
  • Initial customer feedback
  • Defined value proposition
LEVEL 2

PoC to Product Market Fit

Focuses on refining the product, validating the market, and preparing for commercialization.

Support Offered:

  • Advanced Technical Mentoring
  • Product Development Assistance
  • Market Validation Studies
  • Customer Discovery Programs
  • Access to Wet Labs & Testing Facilities
  • Branding & Marketing Support
  • Business Planning & Industry Connect
  • Fellowship Opportunities

Expected Outcomes:

  • Product-Market Fit validation
  • Initial customer traction
  • Pilot deployments
  • Revenue Model Development
LEVEL 3

PMF to Revenue Generation

Supports startups in scaling their operations and establishing sustainable businesses.

Support Offered:

  • Growth Strategy Development
  • Market Linkage Support
  • Investment Readiness

Expected Outcomes:

Virtual Incubation (3 & 6 Months Tracks)

Supporting Innovators Beyond Geographical Boundaries. Designed for innovators and startups who cannot regularly access the incubation center physically due to distance or operational constraints. The program provides structured incubation support through digital platforms while maintaining access to mentors, experts, and business support services.

Ideal For:

Rural Innovators Student Entrepreneurs Early-Stage Startups Researchers Working Professionals Women Entrepreneurs Community-Based Enterprises

Key Program Features

  • Mentoring Support: One-on-One Mentorship Sessions, Technical Guidance, Business Strategy Reviews, and Milestone Progress Monitoring.
  • Capacity Building: Online Workshops, Design Thinking Programs, Business Planning Sessions, and Market Research Training.
  • Expert Access: Instant query routing to Sector Specialists, Technical Experts, Industry Mentors, and Legal & Financial Advisors.
  • Market Linkages: Customer Introductions, Industry Networking, Market Intelligence, and Partner Connect Programs.

Eligibility & Priority Sectors

ACIC-JPIF welcomes applications from individual innovators, early stage startups, community groups (SHGs, FPOs), students, researchers, and women entrepreneurs across Maharashtra and India.

Priority Innovation Sectors:

Bamboo Ecosystem Agriculture & Allied Activities Agri Biotechnology Medicinal Plants & Ayurveda Renewable Energy Sustainability Food Processing Rural Livelihoods Social Innovation

Selection Process

  • Stage 1: Application Screening: Evaluated based on Problem Relevance, Innovation Potential, Community Impact, and Feasibility.
  • Stage 2: Preliminary Review: Applications are reviewed by the ACIC team and domain experts.
  • Stage 3: Presentation / Pitch: Applicants present their Problem Statement, Proposed Solution, Business Opportunity, and Expected Impact.
  • Stage 4: Expert Evaluation: Assessment by Sector Experts, Industry Mentors, Business Advisors, and the Incubation Team.
  • Stage 5: Due Diligence: Verification of Team Capability, Technical Feasibility, Market Opportunity, and Scalability.
  • Stage 6: Final Selection: Selected applicants receive an Offer Letter, Incubation Agreement, and Onboarding Support.
